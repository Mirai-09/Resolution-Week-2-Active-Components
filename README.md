# Resolution-Week-2-Active-Components
This is a circuit that I designed for Week 2 of Resolution Hardware
My circuit has LEDs that blink when a switch is pressed as well as turn on for a few seconds and then turn off.  

# How it works
This circuit has two Multivibrators in it. The one on the left is a Astable Multivibrator. It makes the LEDs on the left blink on and off when the switch is turned on. The one on the right is a Monostable Multivibrator. When the switch on the left is pressed and released, the LEDs turn on for a few seconds and then turn off. I have made thi circuit with the help of transistors, capcitors and resistors.
